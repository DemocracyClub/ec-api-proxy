The Electoral Commission provides access to a year-round election lookup and polling station finder, powered by a new election information API and delivered in partnership with Democracy Club.

This tool helps people quickly find out which elections are taking place in their area, providing details of their polling station and local council addresses. The lookup is widely used, for example during the 2019 General Election it handled 3.5 million postcode searches in election week alone.
